Why ABM is Different:

  • Multi-stakeholder engagement ‚Äì reaching executives, purchasing, and managers simultaneously.
  • Personalization at scale ‚Äì tailored communication based on industry and pain points.

Why Our Emails Don't Land in Spam

Email deliverability is crucial for successful outreach. We employ several key strategies and technologies to ensure your emails reach the inbox and maintain a strong sender reputation:

  • Email Warm-up: New email accounts are gradually "warmed up" by sending a low volume of emails initially and slowly increasing it over time. This process builds trust with email service providers, signaling that the emails are legitimate and not spam.
  • Email Verification (FullEnrich): Before sending, all email addresses are meticulously verified using tools like FullEnrich. This ensures that we are sending emails to valid and active inboxes, drastically reducing bounce rates and protecting your sender reputation. Sending to invalid addresses can severely damage your domain's reputation and lead to emails being marked as spam.
  • Advanced Personalization (Clay & Custom AI): Especially in our advanced offer, we leverage AI-powered personalization through platforms like Clay and our custom AI for the Russia market. Sending highly personalized and relevant emails significantly increases engagement and reduces the likelihood of being flagged as spam, as recipients are more likely to open and interact with content tailored to them.
  • Reputation Management (Instantly): Our email sending platforms like Instantly are configured to manage sending volume and frequency effectively across multiple accounts and domains. This distributed sending strategy, combined with continuous monitoring, helps maintain a healthy sender score.
  • USA IP Usage: We utilize USA-based IP addresses for sending emails. This practice enhances deliverability and reduces the likelihood of emails being flagged as spam, as USA IPs are generally considered more trustworthy by email service providers.
  • Highly Targeted Lead Lists & Content Alignment: Our lead lists are meticulously curated to ensure they consist of highly targeted prospects whose profiles align perfectly with the content of our emails (Ideal Customer Profile - ICP alignment). This relevance drastically increases engagement rates and signals to email providers that our communications are valuable, not spam.

By combining these strategies, we ensure high deliverability rates, allowing your messages to consistently reach your target audience's inboxes.
